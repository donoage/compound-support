Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 8, 2025

Summary: Compound. is designed with privacy in mind. We don't collect, store, or share your personal or financial data. All calculations happen locally on your device.

Information We Don't Collect

Compound. does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We don't collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We don't collect or store your financial calculations
  • We don't track your location
  • We don't create user profiles or accounts

Local Data Storage

Any calculations you save within the app are stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Compound. uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements in the free version. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • Apple StoreKit: Processes in-app purchases securely through Apple's payment system.

Children's Privacy

Compound. does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences interested in financial calculations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
